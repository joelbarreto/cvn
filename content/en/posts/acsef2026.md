### <span class="green">Wisdom in STEM in Action</psan>

Caring Valley advanced its Wisdom in STEM mission at the Alameda County Science & Engineering Fair by supporting the event through experienced volunteer judges and by securing Hitachi as the grand title sponsor for the joint Science Fair and Community Initiative.

Our involvement focused on strengthening the fair’s educational impact: seasoned judges offered thoughtful evaluation and mentorship to student innovators, while the new title sponsorship expanded resources for both ACSEF and the broader community STEM effort.
<!--more-->   

Through this partnership, Caring Valley helped elevate the visibility, stability, and reach of STEM learning across Alameda County—ensuring students benefit from both expert guidance and a stronger, community‑powered foundation for future fairs.

### ABOUT THE PROGRAM
*Wisdom in STEM* is a community initiative that brings together **adults aged 50+ with technical or scientific backgrounds** and **middle & high school students** exploring hands‑on science.

Our volunteers serve as:
<ul>
✔ Science Fair Judges <br>
✔ STEM Mentors <br>
✔ Career Storytellers <br>
✔ Workshop Leaders <br>
</ul>
Whether for one day or several months, every volunteer helps build a bridge of learning, curiosity, and connection.
