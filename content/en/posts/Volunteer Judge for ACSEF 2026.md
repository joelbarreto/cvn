Caring Valley's <a class="b link" href='{{< relref "wisdom-in-stem.md">}}'>Wisdom in STEM program</a> invites you to lend your invaluable experience as a volunteer judge for the **2026 Alameda County Science &amp; Engineering Fair (<a class="link" href="https://www.acsef.org/">ACSEF</a>)**. <!--more-->
**Fair Date:** Saturday, March 14, 2026  
**Location:** Chabot College, Hayward, CA  
**Commitment:** Approximately 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM (includes orientation, judging, and lunch)

---

### **Why Register at Caring Valley?**

When you register as an ACSEF judge through Caring Valley, your participation creates **two powerful outcomes:**

1. **Direct Student Mentorship:** Engage with talented middle and high school students, provide constructive feedback on their projects, and inspire their passion for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
2. **Crucial Fair Sponsorship:** For every judge who signs up via Caring Valley, we will **fully sponsor ACSEF** for the administrative and operational costs associated with your judging role. This means your time directly contributes vital financial resources to the fair, helping to keep student registration fees low and ensure ACSEF remains a high-quality event for all.

**Already an ACSEF Judge?** Your participation is still incredibly valuable! By signing up through Caring Valley, you unlock direct financial support for ACSEF, strengthening the fair's resources. It's a simple way to amplify your existing commitment.

---

### **Who We're Looking For**

We welcome professionals, academics, and enthusiasts from all STEM fields, including:

- Engineers (Software, Mechanical, Electrical, Environmental, Civil)
- Scientists (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science, Data Science)
- Medical Professionals
- Technology Innovators
- Educators with STEM backgrounds

---

### **Judge Responsibilities**

- Attend a brief online or in-person orientation (details provided upon registration).
- Review assigned student projects.
- Interview students about their research, methodology, and findings.
- Provide constructive feedback and assign scores based on ACSEF criteria.
- Collaborate with other judges to select finalists.
